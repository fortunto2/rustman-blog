The original dream, started in 2014: help people turn everyday video moments into meaningful stories. Not just random clips — a narrative, a reflection on life.

Closed v1 in 2021 (technology wasn't ready). Now rebuilding as iPhone-first app with on-device AI: automatic highlight detection, beat-synced editing, intelligent story arc construction. Zero cloud dependency.

**What it does:** import videos → AI selects best moments → beat-sync to music → export montage. All on-device.

**Stack:** Swift + SwiftUI + AVFoundation + Apple Foundation Models + MLX. On-device video analysis, no cloud processing.

**Status:** in development (v2, iPhone-first).

**Why it matters:** this is the origin story. "Every product I build is a step toward that original vision: tools that help people reflect, create, and own their narrative." SuperDuperAI exists because Life2Film planted the seed.
