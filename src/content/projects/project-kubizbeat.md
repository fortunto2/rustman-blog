# Jaw Harp Synth (KubizBeat)

Virtual jaw harp (kubyz/vargan) synthesizer for iOS. Play traditional instrument sounds with modern digital effects on your iPhone.

![KubizBeat](/images/projects/kubizbeat-icon.png)

## Sound Engine

Physics-based synthesis capturing the overtone-rich timbres of real jaw harps. Multiple tuning presets from various regional traditions:

- **Bashkir kubyz** — the original inspiration, warm resonant overtones
- **Yakut khomus** — bright, metallic character
- **Vietnamese dan moi** — delicate, high-pitched harmonics

## Interface

- **Touch controls** — tap to pluck, slide for pitch bending
- **Multitouch** — overtone selection and resonance shaping
- **Designed for expressive playing** — not a toy, a real instrument

## Effects

Built-in reverb, delay, and modulation. Range from traditional acoustic timbres to experimental electronic textures.

## Recording & Sharing

Export as WAV or MP3. Share directly to SoundCloud, Instagram, TikTok. DAW import for music production.

## Why This Exists

Intersection of personal roots (Bashkortostan), technology, and cultural preservation. The kubyz is a traditional Bashkir instrument that most young people have never played. This app makes it accessible to anyone with an iPhone — no instrument needed, no teacher needed.

## Stack

Swift + SwiftUI + AVAudioEngine. On-device audio synthesis, no server, no internet required. [[privacy-as-architecture|Privacy-first]] — no registration, no data collection.
