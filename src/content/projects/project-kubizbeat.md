iOS app for learning and playing the kubyz (Bashkir jaw harp / vargan). Digital instrument + learning tool preserving cultural heritage through technology.

**What it does:** digital kubyz instrument with authentic sound synthesis, learning sequences for traditional techniques, recording and sharing.

**Stack:** Swift + SwiftUI + AVAudioEngine.

**Why it matters:** intersection of personal roots (Bashkortostan), technology, and cultural preservation. Teacher by education, activist by experience — this connects both.
