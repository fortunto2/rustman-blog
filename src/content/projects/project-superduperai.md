## Revenue Model

**Consulting:** Epiphan Video (Canada) -- video capture, streaming, computer vision. Consulting funds product experiments.

**Barbell strategy:** 90% safe income (consulting) + 10% product bets. Each product is an experiment. Revenue is feedback, not the goal.

## Company Structure

**Turkey (SuperDuper AI Teknoloji):**
- Tech park residency (Antalya Teknokent)
- R&D tax benefits (0% KDV for software export)
- Tech Visa

**USA (SuperDuperAI Corp):**
- Delaware C-Corp
- For US market products and investor optionality

**Location:** Antalya, Turkey. Exploring: Cyprus, Portugal for residency optionality.

## Philosophy

- **Privacy-first:** on-device AI, user data never leaves the device
- **One pain, one feature, launch:** each product starts from a single real problem
- **Portfolio approach:** diversify bets, not eggs in one basket
- **Offline-first:** works without internet, syncs when available
- **Open source as portfolio:** crates and tools build reputation, attract contributors
