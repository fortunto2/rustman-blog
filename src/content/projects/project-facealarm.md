iOS app born from real life: wife runs a face massage coaching business, needed a timer with guided exercises. No good app existed — built one.

**What it does:** guided face massage timer with exercise sequences, AI-powered form feedback (on-device via Apple Foundation Models), progress tracking. Privacy-first: all data stays on device.

**Stack:** Swift + SwiftUI + StoreKit 2 + CoreML/Apple Foundation Models. On-device AI for form analysis — zero API cost.

**Status:** active, App Store live.

**Lessons:**
- Products from life = real pain, not hypothetical
- iOS utility apps: $2-5K MRR ceiling (hardest niche to monetize)
- On-device AI removes the cost barrier for AI features in freemium
