Taleb's antifragility applied not to investments but to **life design** across 5 domains: income, health, relationships, skills, location. Three levels per domain:

| Domain | Fragile (avoid) | Antifragile (aim for) |
|--------|---|---|
| Income | Single employer | Revenue that grows from volatility |
| Health | Stress avoidance | Controlled stressors (fasting, HIIT) |
| Relationships | Dependence on one person | Moai group that strengthens from crises |
| Skills | Narrow specialization | Skill combinations |
| Location | Tied to one place | Residency optionality |

**Barbell strategy:** 90% in safe + 10% in experiments with unlimited upside. This is NOT diversification (which Taleb calls "protection from ignorance"). Barbell is deliberate concentration at the extremes, not in the middle.

For startups: barbell = consulting (safe 90%) + solo products (10% with potential upside). Consulting funds experiments, experiments can become the main income.
