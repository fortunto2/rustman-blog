## Key Claims

Operational playbook in 11 sections. Core: idea → live product with traffic in 5-9 days.

**5D Contour:** Discovery → Decision → Design → Delivery → Distribution. Each phase 1-3 days.

**S.E.E.D. scoring:** Searchability (can I rank?), Evidence (real pain), Ease (MVP in 1-2 days), Demand (will they pay?). Kill flags: giants in top-10, fresh guides, no evidence of real pain.

**Fake-Door test:** Landing + $20 traffic. < 10 clicks = KILL. 10+ = proceed.

**Kill/Iterate/Scale:** Week 1 — fake-door clicks, week 2 — signups (0=kill, 5+=scale), week 3+ — WoW growth (<0%=kill, >50%=scale).

**Founder bandwidth:** if total > 40 hours → bloating into platform.
